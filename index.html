Editor

  • Choose a already folded model in the dropdown. Skip through the steps with the slider. Notice the creasing view, this shows how the unfolded paper would look like.
  • Fold by your own. You can activate the ruler with key 't'. Then drag the mouse you will see preview. After placing the plane you can choose to crease only, reflect or fold.
  • Reflect & Fold will always act on all polygons that are involved. But you can select the face by double clicking on the according Polygon in the Creasing View.
  • You are not able to fold if the paper would tear apart. It iwll only crease in that case.
